Kollam

Kollam is an ISO 9001:2015 certified city corporation for the best Municipal administration and services.As per the survey conducted by Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU) based on urban area growth during January 2020, Kollam became the 10th fastest growing city in the world with a 31.1% urban growth between 2015 and 2020. It is a coastal city on the banks of Ashtamudi Lake that took the title God's Own Country.
